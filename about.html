ABOUT US

V I C T O R I A ' S


VICTORIA'S was founded in 2023, in TARUMT College Penang Branch , with the mission to bring you locally-designed jewelry pieces for your everyday wear.

Every piece is thoughtfully designed from scratch, and uses our high-quality diamond simulants.

Our pieces shine with the same level of brilliance and luxury of real, naturally-mined diamonds, all without the hefty price tag.
